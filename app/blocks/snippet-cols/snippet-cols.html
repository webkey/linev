An innovation project portfolio (IPP) is the source of innovation, the perpetual motion machine of an innovation ecosystem. IPP 30/30/30/10 is balanced, reflects a corporate innovation strategy and focused on four innovation horizons:

- incremental innovation (30%), aimed at continuous improvement of serial products to increase its value in the market; - architectural innovation (30%), aimed at changing the overall design and key components of products to solve the customer's problem, exceeding his expectations. These are usually completely new markets; - disruptive innovation (30%), aimed at creating fundamentally new technologies and products that are an effective alternative to products already existing on the market; - radical (breakthrough) innovation (10%) innovations aimed at creating a new product, process or service with a high technological component.
